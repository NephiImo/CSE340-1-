Additional Enhancement: Saved Vehicles

Description
I added a new Saved Vehicles feature for logged-in users.

This enhancement allows a user to:
- save a vehicle from the vehicle detail page
- view all saved vehicles from their account area
- remove a vehicle from their saved list

Database Interaction
- A new table named account_favorite stores the relationship between an account and a vehicle.
- The table includes:
  favorite_id
  account_id
  inv_id
  favorite_created

What Was Added
- Model:
  models/favorite-model.js
  Handles creating, reading, counting, adding, and removing saved vehicles.

- Controller:
  controllers/favoriteController.js
  Handles building the saved vehicles page and processing save/remove actions.

- Validation:
  utilities/favorite-validation.js
  Validates the selected vehicle before saving or removing it.

- Routes:
  account routes were updated to support:
  /account/favorites
  /account/favorites/save
  /account/favorites/remove

- Views:
  views/account/favorites.ejs
  New page that shows all saved vehicles for the logged-in user.

  views/inventory/detail.ejs
  Updated to include Save To My Account / Remove From Saved Vehicles buttons.

  views/account/management.ejs
  Updated to show the number of saved vehicles and a link to the saved vehicles page.

How To Navigate To The Feature
1. Start the application.
2. Open the site in the browser.
3. Log in to an existing account, or register a new account and then log in.
4. From the home page, click any vehicle classification in the navigation bar.
5. Select a vehicle to open its detail page.
6. On the vehicle detail page, click Save To My Account.
7. Click My Account in the header.
8. On the Account Management page, click View Saved Vehicles.
9. The Saved Vehicles page will display all vehicles saved by that account.
10. If desired, click Remove From Saved Vehicles to delete one from the list.

Notes
- Users must be logged in to save vehicles.
- The feature works for normal client accounts.
- The new database script is also saved in:
  database/additional-enhancement.sql
